Mental Health Title

How are you feeling?

We, as students, know how tough school can get. Assignments, midterms, finals, social life, financial status, etc. are all problems we have to deal with. Fortunately, you don't have to go through it alone. Our university has plenty of resources available that can make your college experience a little easier. You can take an assessment which will recommend you the appropriate sources you may need, depending on your responses. If you do not wish to take an assessment, you can explore the many services available on campus and choose which one you may want to check out.
